    Chat Confidential is an anonymous messaging platform, currently in development, with applications in hospital and counselling settings. It acts a conversation medium connecting qualified consultants with students allowing students to communicate challenges without exchanging personal contact information. Data created through the use of the app can be stored for analysis or destroyed based on the needs of clients. With Chat Confidential we aim to create a community which facilitates connecting students in need with individuals who can help.
